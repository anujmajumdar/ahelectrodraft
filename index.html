ABOUT US

A.H. ELECTRO - an authorized reseller & agent distributor pioneer offering IC's, passive & active, connectors, electromechanical and discrete components.
A.H. ELECTRO is a professional distributor for electronic components, which is focused on value-added services.
Our products include these famous international brands, such as Microchip, ADI, MAXIM, TI, Micro, MURATA, TDK, SAMSUNG, YAGEO, T, JST, ROHM, kyocera, Mitsubishi, XC, ALPS.
A.H. ELECTRO has an experienced team. Our proficient sales team shall provide quality solutions meanwhile the World-wide purchase team source the components globally for the best. A.H. ELECTRO always does business on the ethics of development, mutual benefit which makes all our customers trust us. We are looking forward to withstanding a long-term & stable reliable relationship with you!
